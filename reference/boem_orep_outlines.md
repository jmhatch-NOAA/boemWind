# BOEM Office of Renewable Energy Program Lease and Wind Planning Areas

An `sf` object containing the outlines for BOEM Renewable Energy Lease
(LEASE_STAGE = 'Active') and Wind Planning (LEASE_STAGE = 'Planning')
Areas.

## Usage

``` r
data('boem_orep_outlines')
```

## Format

An `sf` collection with 71 features and 30 fields.

- Geometry type:

  MULTIPOLYGON

- Dimension:

  XY

- Bounding box:

  xmin: -158.6636 ymin: 20.7765 xmax: -68.5727 ymax: 43.9506

- Geodetic CRS:

  WGS 84

## Source

<https://www.boem.gov/renewable-energy/mapping-and-data/renewable-energy-gis-data>

## Details

The Lease Area Boundaries were updated on August 05, 2025 and the Wind
Planning Area Boundaries were updated on August 05, 2025. The Wind
Planning Areas were rescinded on July 30, 2025.
